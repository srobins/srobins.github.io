// 02_BIO.TXT

Who, allegedly

nonrocker (nonrocker Turbolag, Turbolag TurboLag, the matter is unresolved) formed in a Salt Lake basement between the second and third soju. The brief was simple: do not make rock. Make whatever the opposite is, but with more kazoos and slightly more remorse.

The band operates at the intersection of vaporwave, digital hardcore, and the Sunday-morning feeling of waking up next to a Windows 95 install disc. Their debut LP Soju, Kazoos and Didgeridoos is engineered to crash whichever app you play it in.

The rebrand to Turbolag remains in arbitration. The bassist insists it is two words. The drummer insists there is no drummer.
